Featured Project

The Lovers

Dive into "The Lovers," a captivating animated thriller series by a small independent team of young Filipino artists. Set in a dark alternate fantasy Philippines, the series delves into the intense sapphic romance between Sarah Lim Baylon, a seafood chef, and Sirena, a captivating siren. With each frame, this collaborative masterpiece weaves dark fantasy, romance, and suspense, inviting you on a journey into uncharted storytelling realms.

Pandesai

A gifted artist whose passion unfolds across the vibrant landscapes of Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Specializing in sapphic and GL (Girls' Love) art, Pandesai weaves visual tales of love and connection that resonate with the heart. Explore KD Chan's gallery, where each digital stroke weaves a tale of unbounded creativity and captivating imagination.

Marahuyo (adj.)

a Filipino word that means to be attracted or enchanted; often attributed to magic or fantasy, but it can also mean to be enchanted in discovering, in learning something new, and even in falling in love

About the Project

Marahuyo, is a passion-fueled hobby project by Venz Milado from BSIT-2B that serves as a cozy gallery highlighting the exceptional talent of my favorite Filipino artists. This curated space offers a heartfelt glimpse into the diverse world of Filipino artistry that has left an indelible mark on my appreciation. Explore Marahuyo and discover the rich tapestry of creativity that defines these remarkable artists.
